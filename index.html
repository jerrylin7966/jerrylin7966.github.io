ConvoFlow

ConvoFlow is a Chrome extension that captures, summarises, and organises your AI chat sessions across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, Grok, and DeepSeek — so you never lose a useful conversation again.

With ConvoFlow you can inject your saved memory into any AI platform, broadcast prompts across multiple AIs simultaneously, and get a personalised daily brief powered by your Google Calendar and Gmail.
