ConvoFlow Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

ConvoFlow ("we", "our", or "the extension") is a Chrome extension that helps you capture, summarise, and reuse your AI chat sessions. This policy explains what data we access, how it is used, and what we never do with it.

1. Data We Access

  • AI chat content — ConvoFlow reads text from supported AI platforms (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, Grok, DeepSeek) only when you actively trigger a save. This content is used solely to generate a summary stored locally in your browser.
  • Google Calendar — If you connect Google, ConvoFlow reads today's calendar events to show a personalised daily brief. This data is never stored beyond your browser session cache.
  • Gmail — If you connect Google, ConvoFlow reads the subject lines and senders of recent unread emails to surface them in your daily brief. Message bodies are never read.

2. How Data Is Used

  • Chat summaries are stored locally in chrome.storage.local on your device only.
  • Google Calendar and Gmail data is used in real time to generate your Morning Brief and is never written to any external server or database.
  • Summaries are generated by sending content to the Anthropic API using your own API key. Anthropic's privacy policy governs that data.

3. Data We Never Collect

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any ConvoFlow server.
  • We do not read Gmail message bodies — only subject lines and sender names.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising or analytics beyond anonymous usage counts (if enabled).

4. Third-Party Services

  • Anthropic API — used to generate summaries. Subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Google APIs — Calendar and Gmail access via OAuth. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Permissions

ConvoFlow requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage — to save memories locally on your device
  • scripting / activeTab / tabs — to read and inject content into supported AI platforms
  • sidePanel — to display the ConvoFlow panel in Chrome
  • identity — to authenticate with Google for Calendar and Gmail access

6. Data Retention

All memories are stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local. You can delete individual memories or clear all data at any time from the ConvoFlow settings panel. Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data.

7. Children's Privacy

ConvoFlow is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as new features are added. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
